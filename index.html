About Me


Hey, I'm Shaswat Gupta, a 22 year old Information Technology Bachelor's Student at National Institute of Technology in Durgapur, India.

Throughout the three years as an undergraduate student, I've worked on projects in several different fields - Semantic Web Technologies, Data analysis, Cryptography, Robotics, Mathematics and Mobile (Android).

Since last year, I've been deeply invested in all things web-related. I began creating web applications based on django python framework.

In my spare time, I enjoy writing, reading, playing table tennis and swimming. I am a supporter of open-source development and a firm believer in John Resig's write code every day.While commit streaks do not equate to the skill of a developer, they help keep my skills sharp while I work on the every day projects that I love.
